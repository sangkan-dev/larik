# Code of Conduct

Larik follows a simple contributor standard: be respectful, practical, and focused on improving the project.

## Expected Behavior

- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Respect different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Keep critique technical and actionable.
- Assume good intent, but be direct about project constraints.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or discriminatory language.
- Personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Repeatedly derailing technical discussions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or restrict participation when behavior harms the project or its contributors.
